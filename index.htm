Type Detail Info is one part of a much larger Typography website. It is not a separate entry in the 100 day project or the Net Awards competition. Typography enjoys a long, rich history here, and I'm thankful for the opportunity to participate.

Type detail is an ongoing project inspired by the great discontent's 100 day project and Stephen Coles's book The Anatomy of Type. The goal is to annotate a web typeface each day, pointing out the details of the typefaces that one often takes for granted.

Anatomy of a Typeface by typedia explains typography terminology used in this project. If you are interested in joining me on this type exploratory trip, you could grab the template and start annotating your favorite typefaces.
